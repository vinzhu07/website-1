What if I don't want to take on an additional project?

Our club is structured so that this is a very flexible, low-stakes project! We're more focused on developing the necessary skillset for you to be able to start your own venture when you feel ready. The point of the project is to give you a scenario to think critically about how you would apply the skills practially - learning by doing is often more effective than just trying to absorb the knowledge!


Do I have to be randomly matched with a team?

Not at all! If you have team members already, just make sure to indicate who they are in the right place when filling out the Team Matching survey.
