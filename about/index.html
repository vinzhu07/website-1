Rising Entrepreneur Society (RES@GT)

The mission of RES@GT is to promote student entrepreneurship by providing hands-on experience with building startups and navigating the Venture Capital (VC) world. RES@GT aims to foster a community of motivated and innovative individuals who are equipped with the skills and education to lead startups from conception to the growth stage. RES@GT is focused on developing relationships with VC firms, bringing in knowledgeable speakers, and equipping members with the tools to succeed.
